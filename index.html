Get to Know Our Brand

What is R.O.M.E?

R.O.M.E is a clothing brand that sells unisex clothes to promote EQUALITY.

What is your goal?

Our goal is to help those people be more confident with their own choice of clothing while still being comfortable.

How are you unique from other clothing stores?

We have clothes for all including those from the LGBTQ+. Also, we provide products for all body types. We also listen to our customers' opinions about our brand so we can improve our brand in better ways.

Is your brand expensive?

No, we try and make our products affordable so some less fortunate people can still buy from our store if they want. Although our brand is affordable, we tend to make sure that our products have a great quality for our buyers.
